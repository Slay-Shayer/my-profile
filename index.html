High-school Students' Analysis
This project showcases one of my initial forays into Kaggle, focusing on classifying high-school students by ethnicity (Asian, Caucasian, African American) and predicting their performance over time. Through a combination of data visualization and a foundational machine learning model, this work provides early insights into student trends.

Heart Disease
Ethnic, Age, and Lifestyle Determinants. This early Kaggle notebook delves into the cultural and ethnic effectiveness of heart disease, demonstrating how pulmonary function across age groups influences mortality rates. Applying probabilistic theory, the project highlights the significant impact of inherited factors and detrimental habits like smoking on overall health, from lung disease risk to potential cancers.
